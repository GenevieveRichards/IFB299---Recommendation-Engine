EYE OF BRISBANE

The Wheel of Brisbane is known as one of the most spectacular ways to view the city skyline and also the outstanding views along the Brisbane River. This popular attraction is great for young or old tourists and local's to capture amazing pictures and to see Brisbane and South Bank from up high. The Gondolas are enclosed and fully air-conditioned for comfort and seat up to 6 adults and 2 children. There are also VIP Gondolas which last up to 25 minutes, more comfortable seats, tinted windows and also your choice of personally selected music.
The wheel provides 360 degree view from the heart of Brisbane, allowing you to also see the South Bank Parklands 60 metres from the air. The experience will be like no other that you could imagine in Brisbane, it will make you want to come back and enjoy it again. 
Opening Times
Monday to Thursday       11am to 9:30pm 
Friday & Saturday            10am to 11pm
Sunday                           10am to 10pm                                                                                                   

The wheel is located in the Parklands at South Bank in front of the QPAC Centre
To book or find out more information pleat visit The Wheel of Brisbane.
